About Our Website

BrainTwins's goal is to allow for collaborative communication between two parties that might not have the same information. Our website utilizes ChatGPT to compare answers of two different entrants to see whether the information is similar or different. Based upon strictness and subject matter that the entrants choose, the amount of information that the entrants learn from each other will change.

In the scope of the UGA community, BrainTwins allows for students to study several subjects together without revealing an overabundance of details. BrainTwins is a great for cultivating collaboration and cooperation skills while adhering to the academic honesty policy through BrainTwin's ability to limit the amount of information given to each party.

BrainTwins is also expanding to more personal and enterprise use. Users can currently discuss topics in media and we are currently working to allow users to create custom rooms with their own topics. We also provide a specialized category for users to see if their idea or project meets the criteria of an employer or company without giving up the details.
